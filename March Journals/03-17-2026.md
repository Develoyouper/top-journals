## 1. What I Learned

- I learn that web development has three part. First is Frontend the what we saw and view in the website. The interaction and functionality of every components. It composeof HTML, CSS, JAvascript

- The fullstack . It is the combination of knowing the frontend and backend. 

- Learning to code is hard and it will be very hard if you have not the right mindset. 
- Fixed mindset is believing you are born loser and you cant learn because you have limitations.
- Growth mindset on the other hand is knowing your failures but you dont quit because you dont learn it. It is having a grit , a passion and perserverance on something you really enjoy despite how hard it is.
